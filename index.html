Welcome to Our Project

Empowering Innovations

BeautyFinder is an innovative platform designed to seamlessly connect beauty professionals with clients, enhancing service discovery and booking experiences in the beauty industry.

Key Features

Why Choose Our Platform

BeautyBook enables clients interested in a professional's services to send their personal information directly through the platform. The beauty professional then receives these details via email, facilitating a personalized communication channel for appointment scheduling.

The platform features detailed profiles of beauty professionals, complete with their services, experience, customer reviews, and ratings, empowering clients to make informed decisions.

Tailored for the Moroccan market with the potential for regional adaptation

About the Project

Inspiration Behind the Innovation

The BeautyFinder project thus falls within the scope of my portfolio project for Holberton School, an institution that encourages its students to undertake ambitious projects and to apply their skills in the development of innovative technological solutions. This project is not just an academic exercise; it represents an opportunity to meet a real market need and to make a positive contribution to the beauty industry, while allowing me to advance in my career as a full-stack developer.
